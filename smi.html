Welcome to SMI

Transforming Lives Through Faith & Action

Sojourners Missions International (SMI) began in 2002 as a follow-up to the evangelistic work we started in Kachia (Southern Kaduna in Nigeria) and its environs earlier. We started especially in the Royal Clinic Annex Kachia where we used to hold prayer meetings and reach the patients that came to the clinic.

Today, we have expanded our reach to multiple communities, providing spiritual guidance, educational support, healthcare assistance, and community development programs that reflect the love of Christ.
